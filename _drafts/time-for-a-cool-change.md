# The Only Constant is Change

I’m excited to announce I have accepted a new position and will be leaving my current role in a couple of weeks. 

It been a great ride at my current place of employment and I’m super thankful for all my current company has done for me. I honestly would not be anywhere close to where I am today if not for their generosity, support, and encouragement. 

Without the encouragement to try new things and fail, I would not be half the employee, technician, or security person that I am today. Ironically, if you aren’t attempting things that you may have enormaous potential to fail at, you will achieve your maximum potenital. 

This is one of my favorite quotes from Thomas Edison, and while there is controversy in the amount, the idea is the same.

> _I have not failed 10,000 times. I have not failed once. I have succeeded in proving that those 10,000 ways will not work. When I have eliminated the ways that will not work, I will find the way that will work._
